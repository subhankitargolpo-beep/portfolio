### The Product Requirements (PRD)

**Objective**: To automate the 80% "grunt work" of presentation creation (research, layout, structuring) while maintaining 100% user creative control.

#### Core Product Pillars
- **Agentic Orchestration**: Specialized agents for **Research**, **Curriculum Mapping**, and **Visual Design**.
- **Privacy-First**: Client-side processing using **IndexedDB** to ensure sensitive corporate or educational data never leaves the user's device.
- **Companion Materials**: Automatically generates worksheets, quiz questions, and speaker notes to provide a 360-degree teaching kit.

---

### Market Research & User Empathy

Interviews with corporate trainers and educators showed that "Presentation Fatigue" is caused by the shift from *thinking* about content to *fighting* with slide layout tools.

**SlidePuppy** addresses this by:
1. **Focus on Narrative**: The AI handles the "Slide 1, 2, 3" structure, allowing the user to focus on the story.
2. **AI Co-Pilot**: Granular commands like "simplify this slide" or "find a diagram for this concept" turn the editor into a conversation.

---

### Technical Architecture

- **AI Core**: Gemini 2.0 Flash for multi-agent reasoning and multimodal search.
- **Canvas Engine**: fabric.js for a robust, browser-based design surface.
- **Data Privacy**: Decentralized storage using IndexedDB and LocalStorage.
- **Impact**: Reduced end-to-end presentation creation time for complex educational topics from 4 hours to 12 minutes.
