      My career is driven by one core belief: the best learning products are built on a deep empathy for the user. After 8+ years on the front lines of education designing curricula for global standards (Cambridge, CBSE, IB, ISC), I discovered my passion wasn't just creating content, but shaping the AI-powered products that deliver it.

I now function as the primary bridge between our academic and engineering teams, translating deep user feedback from educators into actionable technical requirements. I coordinate the User Acceptance Testing (UAT) lifecycle, manage bug tracking, and collaborate directly with developers to improve the quality and pedagogical value of our LMS.

I also spearheaded the PRDs and prototypes for future **AI-integrated features**, such as automated question generation and **AI coding tutor labs**, coordinating the entire development process without a formal dev team. Additionally, I've authored **1,000+ hours of Physics content** aligned to Cambridge IGCSE, AS & A-Level standards.

      My product craft is further deepened through building and shipping **4 independent SaaS/AI products**, including **DaikuFi, GraphGPT, SlidePuppy, and an LMS Analysis dashboard**, covering the full 0→1 journey. With a **Diploma in Programming from IIT Madras** and PM certifications from **IBM and IIM Rohtak**, I combine technical fluency with genuine user empathy to build products that truly matter.
